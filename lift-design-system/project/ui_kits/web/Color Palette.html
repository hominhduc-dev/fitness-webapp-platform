Lift Design System

Color palette

Warm-neutral grayscale carries the interface; a single electric blue accent and three semantic colors are used sparingly. Pure black and white appear only inside accent contexts.

Neutrals · “Ink”

Slightly warm grays. Backgrounds breathe near the top of the ramp; foregrounds carry contrast at the bottom.

Accent · Electric blue

One blue, used only for active sets, the primary CTA, focus rings and charted PRs. Never decorative.

Semantic

Success = a completed set. Warn = rare cautions. Danger = destructive actions. Each has a soft tint for backgrounds.

Data visualization

Charts reuse accent + ink + success over a neutral track. No multi-hue, no gradients.
